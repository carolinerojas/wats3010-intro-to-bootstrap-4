Who Are We?

The Art of Anxiety was created in 2019. It was originally designed to be an outlet for individuals struggling with any mental condition, struggle that they were facing, particularly those who suffered from anxiety, one of the most common mental disorders.

The true purpose of this website is to help others realize that they are not alone. They are so far from it. In addition to being an outlet for artists to post their feelings and experiences through visual art, this website has helped to establish hundreds of newfound relationships.

Whether you're struggling with something big or small, hoping to become better known as a self-proclaimed artist, or if you're just trying to post some art online that you do in your free time, welcome to the family! We're glad you chose to see what we are about.
